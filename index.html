Organic Chemistry

Organic Chemistry is the study of carbon-containing compounds, especially those found in living organisms. It focuses on the structure, properties, and reactions of molecules like hydrocarbons, alcohols, and acids

Inorganic Chemistry

Inorganic Chemistry deals with compounds that are not based on carbon-hydrogen bonds. It includes the study of metals, minerals, salts, and coordination compounds, covering a wide range of elements from the periodic table
